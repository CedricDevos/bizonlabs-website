Technology

Bizon Labs developed the only technology that precisely tunes lipid nanoparticle critical quality attributes, including particle size and morphology—enabling applications previously out of reach.

Improved in vivo expression

Up to 3× higher in vivo expression enabled by precise control of LNP particle size and morphology (n = 7).

Precise LNP engineering

Any LNP size between 40–450 nm with controlled morphology (bleb fraction) in minutes.

Rapid scale-up

Scale-up from microfluidic mixers to pilot-scale jet mixers in days instead of months.

Access to Predictive Models

Process models that predict development and manufacturing success or failure.
